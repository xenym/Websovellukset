Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 1 Avocado
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Pepper
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and add salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture on the toasted bread.
  4. Serve immediately.

Vegetarian Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2, chopped Bell pepper
  • 1/4, chopped Onion
  • 1/2, chopped Tomato
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the chopped vegetables until tender.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables and cook until the omelette is set.
  4. Fold the omelette in half and serve.

Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 4-5 Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Peel and slice the banana.
  2. Add the banana, milk, honey, and ice cubes to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve.
