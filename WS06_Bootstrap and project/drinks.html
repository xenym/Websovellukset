Drinks

Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Lemon juice
  • 4 cups Water
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • as needed Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice, water, and sugar.
  2. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add ice cubes to the pitcher.
  4. Serve chilled.

Vegetarian Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Spinach
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Almond milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 4-5 Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Add the spinach, banana, almond milk, honey, and ice cubes to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve.

Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup, chilled Brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • as needed Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. In a glass, combine the chilled coffee, milk, and sugar.
  2. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add ice cubes to the glass.
  4. Serve immediately.
