Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 shredded Cooked chicken breast
  • 2 cups chopped Lettuce
  • 1 cup halved Cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 sliced Cucumber
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Pepper

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber.
  2. Add the shredded chicken to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately.

Vegetarian Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 2 tbsp Hummus
  • 1/4 sliced Cucumber
  • 1/2 grated Carrot
  • 1/4 sliced Bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup Spinach

Steps:

  1. Spread the hummus over the tortilla.
  2. Layer the cucumber, carrot, bell pepper, and spinach on top of the hummus.
  3. Roll up the tortilla tightly.
  4. Slice the wrap in half and serve.

Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked Pasta
  • 1 cup halved Cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 sliced Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup sliced Olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled Feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Red wine vinegar
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Pepper

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and olives.
  2. Add the crumbled feta cheese to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
