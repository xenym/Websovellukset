Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1, chopped Apple
  • 1, sliced Banana
  • 1 cup Grapes
  • 1, segmented Orange
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the apple, banana, grapes, and orange.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lemon juice.
  3. Pour the dressing over the fruit and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.

Vegetarian Quesadilla

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 1/2 cup, shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup, cooked Black beans
  • 1/4 cup, cooked Corn
  • 1/4, chopped Bell pepper
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Place the tortilla in the pan and sprinkle half of the cheese on one half of the tortilla.
  3. Add the black beans, corn, and bell pepper on top of the cheese.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the vegetables.
  5. Fold the tortilla in half and cook until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is golden brown.
  6. Cut into wedges and serve.

Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Granola
  • 1/2 cup Mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Steps:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer half of the Greek yogurt.
  2. Add a layer of granola and mixed berries.
  3. Drizzle with honey.
  4. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately.
